## 交换群的定义

$$
\abgroup(G) := \Field{(\and)}{\;\;}{
    & G[+] : G[\S] \times G[\S] \to G[\S]\\
    & \\
    & (+) := G[+]\\
    & a + b = b + a\\
    & (a + b) + c = a + (b + c)\\
    & 0_{@G} + g = g\\
}
$$
