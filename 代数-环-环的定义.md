## 环的定义

$$
\text{ring}(R,+,\cdot,0,1):=\Field{
    (\and)
}{\;\;}{
    & \text{ab-group}(R,+,0)\\
    & \text{monoid}(R,\cdot,1)\\
    & a\cdot (b + c) = a \cdot b + a \cdot c\\
    & (a + b) \cdot c = a \cdot c + b \cdot c\\
}
$$
