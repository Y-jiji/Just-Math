## 群的定义

$$
\text{group}(G):=
\Field{(\and)}{\;\;}{
    \\
    & (G, \cdot,1) := (G[\S],G[\cdot],G[1])\\
    \\
    & (\cdot) : G\times G \to G\\
    \\
    & (g \cdot h) \cdot k = g \cdot (h \cdot k)\\
    \\
    & 1 \in G\\
    \\
    & 1 \cdot g = g = g \cdot 1\\
    & \forall g\in G:\exist h\in G: g\cdot h = 1\\
    \\
}
$$
