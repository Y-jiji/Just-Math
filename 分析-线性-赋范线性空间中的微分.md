# 赋范线性空间中的微分
## 全微分的定义

$$
\begin{matrix}
\Dif_{@ \NL(U, V)} f :: \Prop{(\and)}{\;\;}{
    & \text{approx}(x)(\phi) 
    := \Prop{(\and)}{\;\;}{
        & \phi \in \NL(U, V)[\S]\\
        & \lim_{@ U;\, (h \to 0)_{@ U}} 
            \frac{\|f(x + h) - f(x) - \phi(h)\|_{@ V}}{\|h\|_{@ U}} = 0\\
    }\\
    & A := \bigg\{
        x \in \Interior_{@ U}(\dom f) : 
        \exist \phi: \text{approx}(x)(\phi) 
    \bigg\}\\
    & \Dif_{@ \NL(U, V)} f: A \to \NL(U, V)[\S]\\
    & \text{approx}\Big(x\Big)\Big(\Dif_{@ \NL(U, V)} f(x)\Big)
}\\
\begin{aligned}
    & ::: \normspace(U)\\
    & ::: \normspace(V)\\
    & ::: f : U[\S] \to V[\S] 
\end{aligned}
\end{matrix}
$$
