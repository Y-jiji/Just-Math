## 连续函数的定义

$$
\begin{matrix}
\underset{@(X, \tau_X)(Y, \tau_Y)}{\mathscr C}(E) := 
    \left\{f:A\to Y:\Field{(\and)}{\;\;}{
        & A \supe E\\
        & \forall O\in \tau_Y:\hat f^{\diamond-}(O)\in \tau_X
    }\right\}
\\
\begin{aligned}
    & :::\text{top-space} (X,\tau_X)\\
    & :::\text{top-space} (Y,\tau_Y)\\
    & ::: E\sube X
\end{aligned}
\end{matrix}
$$
