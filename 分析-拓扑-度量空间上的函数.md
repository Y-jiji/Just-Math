# 度量空间上的函数

## 函数空间的定义

\<形式定义\>

$$
\FnMetrSpace(X, Y) :: \Field{(\and)}{\;\;}{
    & Z := \FnMetrSpace(X, Y)\\
    & \metrspace(Z)\\
    & Z[\S] = \Big\{f: X \to Y[\S]\Big\}\\
    & d_{@ Z}(f, g) = \sup_{@ \R}\Big\{d_{@ Y}(f(x), g(x)) : x\in X\Big\}\\
}
$$
